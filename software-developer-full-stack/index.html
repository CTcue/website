Software Developer in Amsterdam

CTcue

70% of health data is entered as text into the Electronic Health Record (EHR) and their potential remains mostly unused. Manually searching for patients against quality and study criteria remains a major hurdle and adds immensely to the burden of hospitals. Using information retrieval, natural language processing and AI technologies CTcue unlocks the value of this health data.

We are a start-up team with 5 developers (and a hustler) with different expertises and backgrounds. The success of our company now allows us to search for more smart people who are excited about realizing the right stuff. Some examples of technology we work with are: Node.js, Angular.js, D3.js, Python, ElasticSearch, and MongoDB, neo4j

Our head office is at the Science Park in Amsterdam (within incubator Ace Venture Lab) and we are getting started in Birmingham UK. We have close connections to the Amsterdam universities, we work with professors and researchers in the field of information retrieval, Artificial Intelligence and Medical Informatics.
