“The current EHR's are not designed to ensure data can be stored unambiguously, and as a result extracted..."

Translated from “Registratie aan de bron” The Netherlands Federation of University Medical Centres (NFU April 2013)

Searching electronic health records remains a major hurdle. Since most valuable patient information is archived as text (clinical notes, transfer notes, multidisciplinary meeting notes, discharge letters, etc.) into the EHR, it is not possible to automatically search these criteria. Manual search through unstructured patient records is very time-consuming.

CTcue

CTcue is an intelligent information retrieval system for electronic health records. It's an intuitive interface that makes smart use of existing ontologies and automatically adds synonyms, allowing doctors and researchers to recall information from EHR with high precision. CTcue searches the entire EHR, including the text fields, which are interpreted by using the latest innovations in natural language processing and machine learning technologies. The results are instantly available and can be effectively reviewed in detail without having to consult the EHR.

Compliance

Our application is based on “privacy by design”. Prior to entering patient data in our search engine, we pseudonymize all data, including documents, decursus, reports, etc. Our application offers an on site search on non-identifiable data, which allows different hospital employees to browse safely through the EHR data.

Did you know that by manually searching through the EHR, your employees might be violating the privacy act (Dutch Wbp)?

EY’s HvG specialized privacy attorneys validated our process, especially with respect to the 2016 legislation about the Bill on Data Breach Notifications (“Meldplicht datalekken”).

Early adopters

The proof of the pudding is in the eating. The following hospitals are the bravehearts who dared to start with us.

UMCU supported us to develop a proof of principle and became our innovation partner. The unlocked data came from their Research Data Platform (RDP) which extracts data from ChipSoft EZIS.

By unlocking the Epic data, we help researchers and doctors in Spaarne Gasthuis search through their EHR data.

Having its own homebrew EHR Eridanos we let researchers and doctors at Isala search through all their patient data.

LUMC Medical Information (MI) platform provides data which is extracted from ChipSofts Ezis. CTcue searches on this data platform.

The Chipsoft EZIS EHR data at Diakonessenhuis is used as input for our search engine and lets researchers and doctors search through their data.
