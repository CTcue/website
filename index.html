Bringing cutting edge innovation to healthcare

Perplexed by the discovery that clinicians can not easily search through their patient data we made it our mission to facilitate this. We bring the latest in information retrieval, natural language processing and machine learning to enable you to get the most value out of your Electronic Health Records. We wrap this in very user friendly interfaces so clinicians can use it without having to be an SQL database administrator on the side.
