Find patients in an instant

Searching through medical records for patients matching specific criteria is a laborious and time-consuming process.

Our intelligent search engine makes the information you need instantly available

Complex data made easy

Retrieving information from EHR's is difficult, because healthcare data is complex. Clinicians enter their data in multiple places and in different formats. Valuable information is archived as text (e.g. reports or notes), which means a large percentage of valuable information is unavailable for analysis.

We have developed a solution that organizes EHR data into logical categories and that uses text analytics to make the information in text accessible.

Our solutions

Identify patient populations

With CTcue you don't have to be a data analyst to identify patient populations in the database. Our easy-to-use interface allows you to conduct queries ranging from simple to highly complex.

Report on patient outcomes

Once a population has been identified and validated, CTcue can be used to collect data for the evaluation of patient outcomes. Define a set of parameters and get your results with a single click of the button.

Save time

The current method of manually going through medical records in order to identify populations or to collect information is inefficient and expensive. Using CTcue saves you time, because:

  • You no longer have to go through records with little to no relevance
  • Results are instantly available
  • You can search, view and validate data within one application

Privacy

Our application has been developed using a “privacy by design” approach, which means that privacy is our default setting. Before patient data enters our database on the hospital server it is stripped of all content that can be linked back to the patient.

Specialized privacy attorneys from EY have validated our process, paying special attention to the 2016 legislation Bill on Data Breach Notifications (“Meldplicht datalekken”).
