DevOps Engineer

We're seeking a DevOps engineer to work on and improve the CTcue platform

About CTcue

CTcue is an on-premise privacy-by-design platform that empowers care providers, researchers and information intelligence staff to search in their Electronic Health Record (EHR).

Finding patient data in Electronic Health Records is challenging and time consuming. Challenging, because the data may appear in both structured and unstructured formats. Time consuming, because there are many records and lots of details. To tackle this we developed a user-friendly search engine attuned to and focused on the end user.

Core responsibilities

  • Delivering secure, scalable and optimized infrastructures
  • Company IT infrastructure maintenance
  • Design, implementation and maintenance of remote hospital infrastructure using a pull-based orchestration suite such as Puppet.
  • Kubernetes cluster with CI/CD, development Elasticsearch cluster
  • Uphold high security standards in company processes and infrastructure
  • Software Development Life Cycle infrastructure maintenance
  • Collaborate to conceptualize new requirements, implementations and other infrastructure changes
  • Document the infrastructure and automations
  • Centralised monitoring infrastructure for remote deployments
  • Hardening infrastructure

Requirements

  • At least 5 years of experience managing infrastructure.
  • Experience managing databases such as MSSQL, PostgreSQL or Elasticsearch
  • Solid experience with pull-based orchestration suites such as Puppet
  • Solid understanding of networking and security (IPSEC, L2TP VPN)
  • Experience with Linux server administration (Windows servers are a plus)
  • Experience with with container orchestration (i.e. docker, kubernetes)
  • Experience with distributed infrastructures and virtualized environments
  • Experience with languages like Ruby or Javascript
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Fluent in English and optionally Dutch

What do we offer?

  • €45.000 - 60.000 salary range
  • Environment where all ideas are welcome and help out colleagues where possible
  • Opportunity to develop yourself and try out your own ideas
