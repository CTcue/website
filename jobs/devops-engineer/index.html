Devops engineer

Are you the one to shape and manage the future of automation in health care? In an environment with strong growth we are looking for our future colleague with a passion for system and deployment automation.

Company profile

70% of health data is entered as text into the Electronic Health Record (EHR) and their potential remains mostly unused. Manually searching for patients against quality and study criteria remains a major hurdle and adds immensely to the burden of hospitals. Using information retrieval, natural language processing and AI technologies CTcue unlocks the value of this health data.

We are a start-up team of 13 with 8 developers with different expertises and backgrounds. The success of our company now allows us to search for more smart people who are excited about realising the right stuff. Some examples of technologies we work with are: Node.js, Angular, Typescript, Python, ElasticSearch, MongoDB, MSSQL, Rancher, Docker and many more!

Our office is at the Science Park in Amsterdam. We have close connections to the Amsterdam Universities, we work with professors and researchers in the field of information retrieval, Artificial Intelligence and Medical Informatics.

Your profile

  • Affinity for working in a young, challenging, fast moving environment.
  • Having to configure or deploy the same environment twice? No thanks! You have the ambition to automate and remotely manage these processes.
  • You like to make a difference and an impact in health care.
  • Strong people skills to communicate with IT departments of our clients to prepare and setup the required infrastructures.
  • Creative and open to new approaches and solutions.
  • Solid experience with one or more of the following deployment automation platforms: Chef, Puppet, Ansible, SaltStack
  • You know what the differences and benefits are of each of these platforms. Setting up and managing of environments, manifests, inventories, cookbooks, playbooks, catalogs, facts, grains, masters, agents, minions depending on what architecture we decide to choose in the end. You will get a large degree of freedom here.
  • Strong affinity with and knowledge of industry encryption standard and their implementations, setting up secure communication channels for deployment automation and data transfers.
  • Extensive experience with unix-based environments as well as Windows environments
  • Managing dedicated hosts and clusters, a Rancher environment and a Jira instance to support the team with CI/CD, build automation, deployment and agile management.
  • Considerable experience previously setting up proven environments and managing multiple distributed architectures and clusters.
  • Comfortable with working in ISO 27001 / NEN 7510 certified environments and applying these standards.
  • Applying clean coding practises that are reusable and readable for fellow colleagues.
  • Setting up and working with scaled auditing architectures while designing and adhering to stringent data security compliances.

Required and desired

  • BSc / MSc Computer Science and a critical level of thinking
  • 3+ years work experience
  • A real team player but can also work autonomously and define, prioritise and pick up own tasks.
  • Not afraid of getting your hands dirty.
  • Strong experience with Bash, PowerShell, (No/MS)SQL databases, clustering, CI/CD, YAML/JSON, remote management, deployment automation systems.
  • Experience with Ruby or Go is preferred
  • Preferably you have experience with Elasticsearch
  • Docker container management

If you feel you are up for this challenge, contact us to get a coffee sometime soon. We look forward to meeting you!
