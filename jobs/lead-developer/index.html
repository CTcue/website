Lead developer

Do you want to work for a startup that is innovating the healthcare industry?

Company profile

CTcue is making a difference in healthcare by bringing innovation to a market that is held back by legacy systems. Our challenge is to make the data in Electronic Health Records accessible and usefull, by using smart search and AI technologies. The application itself is kept dainty simple due to the highly appreciated intuitive interface. From our start in 2014 we managed to implement 8 hospitals. In 2017 our goal is to grow to 30 hospitals. We have close connections to the Amsterdam universities, we work with professors and researchers in the field of information retrieval, Artificial Intelligence and Medical Informatics. We are looking for a lead developer who is as excited about realizing innovations in the healthcare sector as we are. Are you interested to join us?

The job

You will be part of our management team and translate the business requirements into the on premise product suite and CTcue cloud service. You have the experience and knowledge to bring our product to the next level. You are familiar and skilled with our current tech stack Node.js, Angular.js, Python, ElasticSearch, MongoDB and Neo4j, but you will bring your technology and experiences to our team. You are now working as a CTO or as a lead developer with the ambition to take the next step in your career. You have an academic degree, fluent in Dutch and understands what is needed in the scale-up phase. Within your team you will be responsible for AI, front and backend. We are dealing with patient data, accordingly you will develop processes and procedures following the ISO/NEN/GCP standards.

Main responsibilities

  • Design, develop, adapt and monitor our tech roadmap
  • Keep our high performance culture of accountability, quality, agility, and fun on the high level it is, which is a challenge when growing!
  • As part of management team you will be part of CTcue’s vision, strategy and execution
  • Have the guts to kill our darlings to defend the technology strategy
  • Maintain and inspire the culture to bring new tools and approaches to CTcue to keep our product and technology leading edge
  • Bring in test, security protocols and procedures to ensure CTcue as reliable and trusted partner
  • Friday team meetings, discuss what you will deliver next week
  • Organize Meetups
  • Friday afternoon drinks with beer and jokes.

Desired skills

  • 7 years in development, 3 years as lead developer and preferable as CTO
  • Strong people and coordination skills, complemented by technical experience
  • Experienced in Agile/SCRUM
  • Proven ability to scale up a product/platform
  • You are open to new approaches and solutions
  • Experience with/interest in developing SaaS solutions
  • You hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science or Artificial Intelligence
  • Experience with Node.js, Angular.js, Python, ElasticSearch, MongoDB, machine learning and natural language processing would be great, but an eagerness to learn is more essential
  • Fluent in Dutch

If you feel you are up for this challenge, contact us to get a coffee sometime soon. We look forward to meeting you!
