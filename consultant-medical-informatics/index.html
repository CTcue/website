CTcue in Amsterdam is looking for a Consultant Medical Informatics

Company profile

We are a start-up team with different expertise’s and backgrounds. Because we are highly successful at this moment, we are looking for more talented employees who are as excited as us about realizing the right stuff. We now work with both academic and top clinical hospitals.

Our head office is located at the Science Park in Amsterdam (within the incubator Ace Venture Lab) and we are getting started in Birmingham UK. We have close connections to the Amsterdam universities, we work with professors and researchers in the field of information retrieval, Artificial Intelligence and Medical Informatics.

Introducing technical innovation in hospitals can be a challenge, we know. But we have done this so often that we know who to talk to and when. Even before implementation, we will accompany during talks and we will handle all the technical meetings. Next to technology we are fast becoming experts on medical data privacy laws. The CTcue platform searches on anonymized patient data and runs exclusively within the hospital's environment. Patient data will always be save. 70% of health data is entered as text into the Electronic Health Record (EHR) and their potential remains mostly unused.

Daily tasks

  • Support implementing new hospitals;
  • At the hospitals supporting researchers and physicians finding in their EHR data;
  • Requirement engineering at hospitals;
  • Meetups;
  • Tuesday team meetings, your moment to represent the medical experts;
  • Working with information retrieval, AI and MI departments of the cooperating universities.

Desired skills

  • You are always open to new approaches and solutions;
  • Ideally you just graduated and have a master’s degree in Medical Informatics;
  • You like to make a difference and an impact in health care;
  • You like to work with medical experts;
  • The start-up DNA is in our blood, which means that we are hands-on and proactive. Own input is not only appreciated, it is required!
