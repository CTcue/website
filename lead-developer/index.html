Lead Developer

Do you have the passion to lead our product team to the next level? Our first product has been released and now we need you to improve the reliability of our systems as we rapidly scale our product and organization.

Company profile

We are making a difference in healthcare by bringing innovation to a market that is held back by legacy systems. Our challenge is to make the data in Electronic Health Records accessible and useful. We have close connections to the Amsterdam universities, we work with professors and researchers in the field of information retrieval, Artificial Intelligence and Medical Informatics.

CTcue is a scale-up. We’re currently working for 8 hospitals, but we expect that number to grow to 30 in 2017. Because we are highly successful at the moment, we are looking for more talented developers who are as excited about realizing innovations in the healthcare sector as we are.

Your profile

You are an experienced developer who understands startup culture. You have the ambition and passion to lead complex projects from concept through implementation. You are capable of keeping a team on track.

Our current tech stack contains Node.js, Angular.js, Python, ElasticSearch, MongoDB and Neo4j, but you will bring your technology and experiences to our team.
